Our Philosophy

A Canadian tax and accounting firm that explains the why.

SH Elevate works with individuals, self-employed people, and incorporated businesses on personal and corporate tax, GST/HST filing, business registration, and planning. We are based in Scarborough and work with clients across the GTA and, remotely, across the country.

Canadian tax has plenty of moving parts — the April 30 personal deadline, the June 15 filing date for the self-employed — though any tax owing is still due April 30 — T2 returns due six months after your fiscal year-end, and the $30,000 threshold that makes GST/HST registration mandatory. We keep track of the dates and the rules so nothing slips.

You will always deal with a real person who knows your file, tells you what a number means, and files it right the first time.
