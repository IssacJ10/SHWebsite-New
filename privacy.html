Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information — handled with the same care we bring to your finances.

SH Elevate Financial Group Inc. ("SH Elevate", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial legislation.

Information we collect

To prepare returns and provide our services, we collect information you provide directly — such as your name, contact details, Social Insurance Number, date of birth, income and expense records, and supporting tax documents (T-slips, receipts, and prior assessments). We also collect limited technical information when you use this website, such as your browser type and pages visited.

How we use your information

We use your personal information only to deliver the services you request: preparing and filing tax returns, corresponding with the Canada Revenue Agency on your behalf, providing advisory services, and communicating with you about your engagement. We do not use your information for unrelated purposes without your consent.

Disclosure to third parties

We disclose your information only where necessary to provide our services — for example, e-filing with the Canada Revenue Agency — or where required by law. We do not sell your personal information. Any service providers we rely on are bound by confidentiality obligations.

How we protect your information

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information, including encrypted document sharing, secure storage, and restricted access on a need-to-know basis. While no method of transmission is completely secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Retention

We retain your records for the period required by the Canada Revenue Agency and applicable law (generally six years), after which they are securely destroyed.

Your rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or withdraw consent (subject to legal and contractual limits) by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
