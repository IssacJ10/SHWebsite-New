What We Offer

Personal Income Tax (T1)

T1 returns for individuals and families — employment income, RRSP and TFSA, tuition, medical and home-office claims. Most returns are due April 30; we file yours on time with the CRA.

02

Corporate Tax (T2)

T2 returns and year-end financial statements for local corporations. Your T2 is due six months after your fiscal year-end — we plan around the small business deduction on your first $500,000 of active business income.

Learn More →
03

GST/HST Filing

Once you pass $30,000 in revenue, GST/HST registration is required. We register your account, file your returns, and claim the input tax credits you're owed on business purchases.

04

Business Incorporation
