How Working With a Remote Tax Accountant Works

Virtual tax services in Canada, the same wherever you are.

1

Book a Video Call

Pick a time that works and we'll talk it through — what you file, what's changed this year, and what slips you'll need. No cost, no obligation.

2

Upload Securely

Send your T-slips, receipts and prior returns through an encrypted portal — no email attachments, no paper in the mail. You upload; only we can see it.

3

We Prepare & Review

We prepare your return, then walk through it with you on video before anything is filed — RRSP and TFSA questions, tuition, medical, home-office claims, the lot.

4

E-File with CRA

Once you approve, we e-file directly with the CRA and confirm it was received. T1 returns are due April 30; if you're self-employed, filing is due June 15 but any balance owing is still due April 30.

Wherever you are in Canada, we're here to help.

Book a free consultation and we'll figure out your return together — Ontario, Atlantic Canada, Alberta, or anywhere in between. New to Canada? We'll handle your first-year filing and get you set up for the GST/HST credit.
