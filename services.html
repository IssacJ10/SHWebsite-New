What We Do

Tax & Accounting Services in Scarborough & Toronto

From your first T1 return to running the books for an incorporated business, SH Elevate handles personal tax, corporate tax, GST/HST, incorporation, bookkeeping and tax planning. We meet clients in person in Scarborough and across the GTA, and file securely for clients anywhere in Canada.

01

Personal Tax Services

T1 returns for salaried employees, self-employed and gig workers, students, retirees, and families. We claim the credits people miss — RRSP and TFSA planning, tuition, medical, and home-office expenses — and file electronically. T1s are due April 30; if you're self-employed you have until June 15 to file, but any balance owing is still due April 30. New to Canada? We'll handle your first-year return and get you set up for the GST/HST credit.

Corporate Tax Services

T2 return preparation for incorporated small and mid-sized businesses. Your T2 is due six months after your fiscal year-end, and we make sure you claim the small business deduction on the first $500,000 of active business income. We also help you decide how to pay yourself — salary, dividends, or a mix — and keep your year-end tidy for the CRA.

03

GST / HST Registration & Filing

Once your revenue passes the $30,000 threshold over four consecutive quarters, you're required to register for GST/HST — and many businesses register earlier to claim input tax credits. We handle registration, pick a reporting period that fits your cash flow (monthly, quarterly, or annual), and file your returns on time so you claim back the tax you've paid on business expenses.

04

Business Incorporation & Registration

Starting out or ready to incorporate? We handle federal and Ontario incorporations, run the NUANS name search to clear your business name, and set up your CRA business number and program accounts — corporate income tax, GST/HST, and payroll — so you're ready to invoice and hire. We'll also walk you through whether incorporating actually makes sense for where your business is right now.

How We Work

How our tax and accounting work actually goes.

1

Discovery

A free first conversation about your situation — whether that's a personal return, a corporate year-end, ongoing bookkeeping, or tax planning before a big decision.

2

Document Gathering

You send your T-slips, receipts, and statements through our secure portal, or drop them at the office. We'll tell you exactly what's needed so nothing gets missed.

3

Preparation

We prepare your return or statements, apply the credits and deductions you qualify for, and flag anything worth planning around for next year.

4

Review & Filing

We walk you through the numbers before anything is sent, then file electronically with the CRA and confirm it's been received.
